Pokemon GO WebQuest

Introduction

Welcome, Pokemon Trainers!

Professor Oak needs your help. To become a real Pokemon Trainer, you must use English to explore the Pokemon GO world. In this WebQuest, you will learn new English words, describe places and Pokemon, and work with your team to create a Pokemon route.

Are you ready to catch Pokemon and learn English at the same time? Let's go!

Task

Your mission is to become a Pokemon Trainer using English.

  • Work in teams and learn vocabulary related to Pokemon GO.
  • Design a Pokemon route with Pokemon, PokeStops and a Gym.
  • Create a poster or digital map and present your Pokemon route in English to the class.
  • Show that you can use English to talk about Pokemon GO.

Process

  1. Vocabulary (Session 1)
    Work in teams of three or four students. Visit the links in the Resources section. Learn the Pokemon GO vocabulary. Create a glossary with at least ten words in English and their meaning.
  2. My Pokemon Route (Session 2)
    Draw or design a map of your school, a park or a city. Add at least three Pokemon, two PokeStops and one Gym. Write simple sentences in English, e.g., "There is a Gym near the park." "You can catch a Pikachu here."
  3. Presentation (Session 3)
    Prepare a short oral presentation. Each team member must say at least one sentence in English. Show your map or poster to the class.

Resources

  • Pokemon GO Glossary (official English)
  • Pokemon GO Vocabulary List
  • Learn English with Pokemon GO
  • Online English Dictionary
  • Poster and map creation tools (Canva)
  • Google Maps

Evaluation

  • Vocabulary: Uses Pokemon GO words correctly in English.
  • Writing: Uses simple sentences correctly such as There is, There are and You can.
  • Teamwork: Works well with the group and participates actively.
  • Oral Presentation: Speaks clearly in English during the presentation. Each student must participate in the final presentation.

Conclusion

Congratulations, Trainers!

You have learned new English vocabulary and practiced speaking English in a fun way. You worked as a team, created a Pokemon route and shared your ideas in English. Now you know that learning English can be an adventure. Keep practicing and catching new words!
